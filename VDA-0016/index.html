ABOUT
The Spanish word “verdad”, meaning truth, perfectly embodies Perry’s newest restaurant -VERDAD True Modern Mexican. With the highest quality ingredients, inspired décor, attention to every detail and every bite, VERDAD is a truly vibrant, artisanal dining experience.
 
 
MENU
VERDAD’s truth is founded in its interpretation of traditional Mexican favorites with a modern twist. Our menu showcases a blend of past and present through signature dishes.

See Our Menu
 
BAR AND PATIO
Experience the fusion of traditional Mexican flavors and innovative mixology at VERDAD’s bar with our extensive selection of tequila, mezcal, wines and signature cocktails & mocktails.
 
 
GROUPS
From family gatherings to corporate events, VERDAD offers three private and four semi-private spaces, and three patios for an intimate, personalized experience. Our attentive staff will ensure every detail is taken care of so you can enjoy.
 
 
SWEET TREATS
VERDAD’s Signature Burnt Milk Candy is a decadent treat that embodies the essence of Mexican confectionary and pairs perfectly with our Mexican Hot Cocoa. Enjoy our treats while dining in or visit our candy case to purchase to-go. Plus, our sweets make great gifts.
 
 
TEQUILA AND MEZCAL TASTING EXPERIENCES
Indulge in our signature flights that showcase the distinct flavors and nuances of beloved Mexican spirits as you savor every sip.
